What is Regex?

Regular Expressions is a formal language that helps us to design a pattern to find specific parts from a text. It is popular in most of the programming languages for text operations and natural language processing steps. It has a lot of rules for patterns that make possible to find any part of texts. It is also used to check inputs if they are in correct form or not.
